---
title: Generate QR Code
description: How to generate and download a QR code for your menu
---

Your customers need a way to access your menu. The easiest way is by scanning a QR code placed on their tables or at the counter.

**To generate a QR code:**
1. Navigate to your **Venue** or **Menu** settings.
2. Find the **QR Code** tab or button.
3. The system will automatically generate a unique QR code linked to your live menu.
4. Click **Download** to save the image to your computer.
5. You can now print this QR code on stickers, table tents, or posters!
