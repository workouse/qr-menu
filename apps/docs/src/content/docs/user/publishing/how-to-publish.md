---
title: How to Publish
description: Step-by-step guide to publishing your menu
---

Once you've made changes to your menu and are ready for customers to see them, it's time to publish.

**To publish your menu:**
1. Go to the **Menus** section or the Menu Builder.
2. Look for the **Publish** button (usually in the top right corner).
3. If you have unpublished changes, this button will be active.
4. Click **Publish**. 
5. Confirm the action if prompted. 
Your menu is now live with the latest changes!
