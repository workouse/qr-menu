---
title: Onboarding
description: How to complete your initial onboarding
---

When you first log in to the QR Menu dashboard, you will be guided through a quick onboarding process.

1. **Organization Details**: Enter your basic organization information, such as name and primary contact.
2. **First Venue**: You will be prompted to create your first venue. A venue represents a physical location (e.g., "Downtown Cafe").
3. **Finish**: Once completed, you'll be taken to the main dashboard where you can start adding menus!
