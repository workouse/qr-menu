Welcome to the QR Menu platform! Our system allows you to easily create, manage, and publish digital menus for your restaurants, cafes, or hotels.

With QR Menu, your customers can view your offerings simply by scanning a QR code with their mobile devices. You can update your menu instantly without needing to reprint anything.

**Key Features:**
- Manage multiple venues
- Create dynamic menus with categories and items
- Multilanguage support
- Instant publishing and updates
