---
title: Manage a Venue
description: How to update venue settings
---

You can update your venue's details at any time to keep your customers informed.

**To manage a venue:**
1. Go to the **Venues** section.
2. Click on the venue you want to edit.
3. Navigate to **Venue Settings**.
4. Here you can change:
   - Name and description
   - Branding images
   - Social media links
   - Contact information
5. Remember to click **Save** after making changes!
