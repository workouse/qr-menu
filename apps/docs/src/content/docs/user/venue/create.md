---
title: Create a Venue
description: How to create a new venue
---

A venue is a physical location for your business. You can manage multiple venues under one organization.

**To create a venue:**
1. Navigate to the **Venues** section from the sidebar.
2. Click the **Create Venue** button.
3. Fill in the required details:
   - Venue Name
   - Description
   - Logo and cover image
4. Click **Save**. Your new venue will now appear in your venues list!
