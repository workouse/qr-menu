---
title: Add a Menu
description: How to add a menu to your venue
---

Menus organize what you offer to your customers. You can have multiple menus (e.g., "Breakfast", "Dinner", "Drinks").

**To add a menu:**
1. Navigate to the **Menus** section or open your Venue dashboard.
2. Click **Create Menu**.
3. Enter a title for your menu (like "Main Menu").
4. Choose whether it should be active immediately.
5. Click **Save**. You are now ready to add categories and items to your menu!
